Bagel



~Makes 4 bagels~


    Ingredients:

  • 1 cup self-raising flour
  • 1 cup 0% fat yogurt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds


    Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (356°F). In a bowl, add all the ingredients and mix well. Divide the mixture into 4 pieces and place them on parchment paper.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the egg yolk with 2 teaspoons of water and brush it over the 4 bagels. Sprinkle with chia seeds and bake for about 25 minutes.
  3. Add avocado and an egg or add salmon with philadelphia
