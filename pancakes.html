Pancakes



~Makes about 8 pancakes~


    Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 ¼ cups (300ml) milk (room temp)
  • 1 egg (large, room temp)
  • 3 tbsp melted butter (plus extra for the pan)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional, but great)


    Instructions:

  1. Mix dry ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt
  2. Mix wet ingredients: In another bowl, whisk the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract
  3. Combine: Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients. Stir gently until just combined. A few lumps are okay — don’t overmix!
  4. Rest the batter (optional, but improves fluffiness).Let it sit for 5–10 minutes
  5. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. Add a little butter. Pour ¼ cup of batter per pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface (2–3 mins), then flip and cook another 1–2 mins until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite toppings, such as fresh fruit, maple syrup, Nutella, yogurt, whipped cream, or even a bit of lemon and sugar
